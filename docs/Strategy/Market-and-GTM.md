# 📈 Market & Go-To-Market

> **Naive "copy US AI to India" no longer works** (the way Uber→Ola did). The AI model is a *commodity*. The moat & arbitrage are in **distribution, localization, proprietary data, and owning a vertical workflow** — not the AI. Internalize this or build a dead wrapper. (Full analysis → [[AI-Startup-Ideas-US-Europe-to-India]].)

## Scope decision
Start with **JEE (Physics + Math)** — the [[Verification-Engine|verifier's]] sweet spot (symbolic/numeric). Then **NEET Physics + Physical Chemistry** (also numerical). **De-prioritize Biology/UPSC** — memorization/essay, not math-verifiable, so they don't use our moat (they're a later RAG product line).

## The B2B2C move (solves content + distribution + data in one)
The hardest non-tech problems are **all solved by coaching-institute partnerships**:
- **Content:** institutes hand us their vetted problem banks.
- **Distribution:** they have students → near-zero CAC; co-brand/white-label.
- **Data:** their students generate our [[Student-Model|step-error flywheel]].
- **Revenue:** per-seat licensing (real money) > thin B2C subs.

Target the **thousands of small/mid coaching centers** who can't build AI and fear PW/Allen/Unacademy. That's the beachhead; direct B2C app is the long tail.

## Pricing
- **B2C:** ₹199–499/mo.
- **B2B:** ₹X/seat/month to institutes (**the core of revenue**).

## Indian rails by default
WhatsApp Business API (not app installs) · UPI/Razorpay (not cards) · vernacular ASR/TTS (Sarvam/AI4Bharat). Cuts CAC, fits the user. → [[Lecture-Companion-Overlay]].

## Stay cheap (live on credits)
AWS Activate, Google/OpenAI/Anthropic for Startups, **Sarvam startup program**, Modal/RunPod → funds 6–12 months of compute. Aggressive **solution caching** (exam problems repeat!) → marginal cost → ~₹0 on repeats. Realistic MVP burn: a few hundred $/mo, mostly absorbed by credits. (Stack/cost → [[A1-Math-Verified-Tutor-Dev-Plan]] §3.)

## Defensibility vs incumbents (PW "Sahi", Unacademy, Google)
Compete on **verified correctness + diagnosis** (their AI isn't verified) and sell to the **long tail of institutes they don't serve**. Be a partner/acquisition target, not a head-on rival. See [[Competitive-Landscape]] + [[Durable-Moat]].
