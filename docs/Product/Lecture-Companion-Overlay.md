# 🎬 Lecture Companion Overlay

> Inspired by **Cluely's overlay**, repointed from *cheating* → *learning*. A visible study companion that sits on top of any video the student is already watching (PW, Unacademy, YouTube), reads the screen + listens, and gives **live notes, live doubt-answering, and live verification** — without pausing or switching apps.

## Why it's not "Cluely for lectures" (it's our moat, applied)
Because our core is a [[Verification-Engine|math verifier]], the overlay does something nobody else can: **when the recorded teacher makes a step error or skips a step, our verifier catches it live and flags it.** That turns our moat into the overlay's reason to exist — not two products bolted together. Content we never had to create becomes *our* surface.

## It's a structural answer to the #1 market failure
The category's killer problem ([[Competitive-Landscape]] Problem 2): **students don't open a separate doubt-bot.** Khanmigo became "a non-event." The overlay **meets the student inside the lecture they're already in** — no "remember to go ask the bot." This is the [[Human-vs-AI-Tutor-Gap|Gap 9 (environment)]] + Gap 5 (observation) answer.

## We skip Cluely's hardest, riskiest part
Cluely's nightmare (and most of its bad press: breaches, lag, anti-cheat arms race) is making the overlay **invisible to others** — needed only for *hiding from an interviewer*. **We have no one to hide from.** A student watching their own lecture needs no screen-share evasion → just a normal floating widget / browser extension. ~90% of the wow UX, a fraction of the cost, zero ethical baggage.

## Sequencing risk (important)
Live audio + live OCR + live verification on someone else's **unscripted** video is the *hard* ([[Verification-Engine|v2]]) tier, not v1. **Don't build this first.** Prove the verifier on the curated bank (Phase 0–1), then add the overlay as the **headline + virality engine** of Phase 1–2 — something free, useful, shareable in JEE/NEET Telegram groups, on top of a moat that already works. See [[Roadmap]].
